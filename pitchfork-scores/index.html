The Best and Worst of Pitchfork

From 1999 to 2016, online music magazine Pitchfork reviewed over 18,000 albums. These graphs break down the reviews that received the top and bottom 5% of scores per genre in this time period. Scores range on a scale from 0 (the worst) to 10 (the best), and are grouped together in the graph by whole number score.

Click a section on the graph to see which works earned those scores.
