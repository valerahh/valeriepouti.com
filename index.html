Recent Projects

Woof Smart Collar

This was a project on which I worked with a team to create mobile and desktop application prototypes to accompany a fictional smart dog collar product.

Prototypes ranged from rough pen and paper sketches to full-fidelity prototypes created using Axure.

We conducted user testing throughout development, continuing to collect research through our mid-fidelity (right) and high-fidelity prototypes (above).

Check out the finished prototypes here and here.

Light Builder - The Wabash Lights

This was a project on which I lead a team to create a mobile application prototype and a conceptual video prototype for the Wabash Lights interactive art installation in Chicago, Illinois.

We worked directly with the client during the course of this project, getting feedback along our journey through the user-centered design process.

Our team produced flow charts, pen & paper sketches, and low, mid, and high-fidelity prototypes using Axure. We also perfomed three iterations of usability testing. Check out our process documentation here.

Check out the finished mobile prototype here, and our conceptual video prototype here.
